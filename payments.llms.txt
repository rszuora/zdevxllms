Key Concepts
- **Electronic Payment Collections**: Automating payment runs against invoices.
- **PCI-Compliant Storage**: Securely vaulting credit card and bank account data.
- **Hosted Payment Pages (HPP) and Payment Forms**: IFrame-based card capture to minimize PCI scope.
- **Payment Gateways**: Native integration with 50+ global gateways (Stripe, Adyen, Chase, etc.).
